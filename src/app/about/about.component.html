About Us

Bismi Delight Caterers is an exclusive catering services provider based in Ponnani and has served events across. Over the past 10 years, we have helped our clients to express their extraordinary style with creativity and imagination, delicious food and great guest experience. Our food reflects our commitment to exceptional quality.

Considering your unique requirements, budget and type of events, our expert team offers you an extensive range of recipes and culinary delights to choose from. Our chefs prepare delicious Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian foods, including Chettinad, Tandoor, North Indian dishes, Live counters, Chinese cuisine and a wide range of Arabian specialities like Mandhi, Majboos, Mashawi, Shawarma etc. With only the very best ingredients, uncompromised Hygiene and well-trained staff, your events are guaranteed to be the exceptional.
